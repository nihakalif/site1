About Catering


Tradition since 1889

Chef Philip Howard of Elystan Street dines regularly at this modern Chinese restaurant in Chennai. “It is a great restaurant,” he says. “I love the people, I love the fact it is so casual and I love the food: It is delicious and interesting, with lots of variety. The dim sum is exceptional.” Favorite dish: Garlic steamed lobster, mung bean noodles, superior soy.
